What We Do

Our mission is to unite together and gear towards providing encouragement, motivation and inspiration for women.

Our objectives:

01 01
Empowerment

We empower and promote sustainable socio-economic independence for African women and girls. Providing them with the knowledge and skills needed to live a sustainable and healthy life.

02 02
Equality

We promote equality and rights of African women and girls at all levels in the society.

03 03
Freedom

We promote sustainable financial, social and economic freedom and maximize decision making power over matters that concern womens lives and livelihoods.

04 04
Enlightenment

We promote access to Health Education (Sexual and Reproductive Health) through seminars and workshops.

About Us

Starlight Women Berlin/Brandenburg e. V. is a registered non-profit association officially founded in the year 2021 in Germany. Our goal is to represent African Women and highlight their role in the society by intensifying their participation in cultural, environmental, economic, social and political issues.
